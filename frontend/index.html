Why you need a Privacy Policy

For some websites or apps, a Privacy Policy must be implemented. In some cases, a Privacy Policy statement is required by law. This depends on the platform you are using, and the information you are collecting.

Your website or application may collect some kind of personal information. That's where we come in and generate a customized privacy policy explaining:

  1. What information you compile and how you compile it
  2. How the information compiled is used
  3. How the information collected is protected
  4. If the information is shared with third parties, and what information is shared.

Using Terms & Conditions

Having a good Terms and Conditions (T&C) is very important for every website or application.

A terms and conditions agreement specifies the rules by which parties must abide if they are to retain certain privileges such as the continued use of a product or service. These agreements are commonly employed by websites to explain what behaviors and activities are allowed and which can get a user banned.

We will take care to clear all the doubts of your current and upcoming users with our highly professional T&C generator.
